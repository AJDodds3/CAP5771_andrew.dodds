This file is an overview of the current status of the Data Dictionary for this project.

- Table: Articles 
    - This table is going to store all the data from my analyst ratins csv from Kaggle
    - Variables:
        - article id: Basically just an index to identify the article record. It does not have meaning for the purpose of building a model
        - headline: This column contains all of the headlines associated with the financial articles that will be used in sentiment analysis
        - url: Link to the actual article 
        - publisher: Name of the publisher or author. This is important for classification I will be doing 
        - published_at: Time of the publication of the article which is important to comparing to yfinance for performance
        - ticker: Name of the stock the article is about, needed for classification
        - Headline Class: Category I made that determines whether the headline is analysis, prediction, price movement, or regular news to help with training down the road
- Table: Sentiment
    - This table is going to store all information gathered from sentiment analysis from each of the articles
    - Variables:
        - article id: Same as the article id in the articles table for mapping
        - sentiment score: Output from the model I end up decided to use from article headline
        - sentiment label: Interpretation of the score for classification
        - method: There are multiple types of sentiment analysis and I may try multiple but have not decided at this time
- Table: Returns
    - I need a table to be able to store ticker performance after all article publication dates so my model can relate stock performance to article sentiment and classify the author
    - Variables: These are all subject to change
        - 7dreturn: Stock price change 7 days after article publication
        - 30dreturn: Stock price change 30 days after the article was published 
        - 6mreturn: Stock price change 6 months after the article was published
