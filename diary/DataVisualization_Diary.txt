This week I came up with a rough draft for what the visualizations are going to look like on the dashboard. I am quite happy
with the direction this project has taken the only thing really that are left is cleaning up my clustering and making the visualizations
more robust as I outined in my data visulization code file. 

The main story that the visualization is trying to comminicate is to be an informational and convenient visual for 
retail investors that are interested in a ticker, do not have a lot of time, and need a breakdown of the ticker and the 
publishers behind it pushing information. I want a person that knows nothing but is interested in apple to go to my dashboard,
understand what type of stock apple is (volatility/whats the public sentiment right now), find information about all the people
covering apple and be able to narrow their research to a few top and trustyworthy publishers. 

The goal behind this is simply that almost all of the publishers that write about tickers are either bought out by hedge funds,
have an alternative motive, are garbage AI generated articles, or are generally clueless. If my dashboard can instantly filter out
the bottom of the barrel publishers so regular people are not led astray then that is an achievement in my opionion.
