This week with data wrangling was really interesting and I am pretty happy with the current state. I expect after we 
talk about exactly how im going to do the modeling/classification I am going to have to change a few things but that
should not be too much. During feature selection I dropped article_id because that has no value for classification. All
that matters is the headline, the stock performance, the author, and the headline class. 

I one hot encoded headline_class 0 for prediction 1 for analysis so the feature can be included and have meaning for modeling.
Additionally, I encoded the publisher size into three distinct buckets 0, 1, and 2 described in data_wrangling_part2.ipynb.
I did not one hot encode because I wanted to preserve the detail that 2 actually is higher than 0 in this case the numbers are meaningful
because higher number is higher article count. Lastly, I created the columns for stock price changes over time intervals
we talked about in class so I can classify the author based on sentiment of the headline and performance of the stock in intervals.
